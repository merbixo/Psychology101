Welcome to Psychology 101!

Embark on a journey into the fascinating world of the mind and behavior. This introductory course explores the fundamental concepts, theories, and research methods that define the field of psychology.

Course Overview

Psychology is the scientific study of behavior and mental processes. Throughout this course, we will cover a broad range of topics, including:

  • The history and major perspectives within psychology.
  • How psychologists conduct research.
  • The biological underpinnings of our thoughts, feelings, and actions.
  • How we learn, remember, and think.
  • How humans develop across the lifespan.
  • What motivates us and how we experience emotions.
  • The nature of personality and social interactions.
  • Understanding psychological disorders and their treatments.

How to Navigate This Course

Use the navigation menu on the left to select a module. Each module covers a specific area of psychology. Within each module, you'll find explanations, examples, and key takeaways.
